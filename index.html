What is tap taster

Ever go to a new bar and order a beer based on the flavor profile listed in the menu, only to have it arrive and taste completely different? Well, you're not alone. Taste is complicated and what is considered good differs from person to person. That's where Tap Taster comes in. Tap Taster is a place to keep your beer tasting note's so you don't have to worry about remembering which ones you liked and which ones weren't so great.

How it works

Taste your beer

Taste your beer, think about the flavors you get as it moves from the front to the back of your tongue.

Enter your notes

Enter your notes by filling out the name of the beer, your flavor rating, and a short description of how it tastes to you. You can even upload a picture so you can recognize the beer later.

Repeat the process

Repeat this process as you try more beers and build your tasting note collection.
